At SINGGLEBEE, we want you to be completely satisfied with your educational purchases. If you're not entirely happy with your purchase, we're here to help.

## 🔄 Refund Eligibility

### Timeframe
- **7 Days:** From date of delivery for physical products

- **30 Days:** For defective or damaged items

### Condition Requirements
- Products must be unused and in original condition
- Original packaging must be intact
- All accessories and manuals must be included
- Digital products must not have been downloaded

### Proof of Purchase
- Original order confirmation email
- Order number or transaction ID
- Receipt or invoice
- Photos of damaged items (if applicable)

### Physical Products
**Books and Educational Materials:**
- Full refund if returned within 7 days
- Shipping costs borne by customer for returns
- Products must be undamaged and resalable

**Educational Supplies:**
- Full refund if unopened and in original packaging
- Partial refund for opened items (based on condition)
- Return shipping costs may apply

### Digital Products
**Downloadable Content:**
- Full refund within 14 days if not downloaded
- No refund once downloaded and accessed
- Technical issues qualify for immediate refund

### Custom Orders
**Personalized Educational Materials:**
- Only refund for manufacturing defects
- No refunds for change of mind
- Must be reported within 48 hours of receipt

## 🚫 Non-Refundable Items

### Digital Downloads
- E-books that have been downloaded
- Audio files that have been accessed
- Video content that has been streamed

### Physical Products
- Items damaged due to customer misuse
- Products returned after 7-day window
- Customized or personalized items (except defects)
- Perishable educational materials

### Services
- Educational consultations after completion
- Custom content creation services
- Shipping and handling charges
